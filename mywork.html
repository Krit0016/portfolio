Three Star Media Website

Writing code from a mockup was a challenging but rewarding experience. It required a lot of attention to detail and precision to ensure that the final product matched the original design. I had to rely solely on my HTML and CSS skills to create a responsive and visually appealing website.

I started by analyzing the mockup and breaking it down into smaller components. This helped me to understand the layout and structure of the design. I then used my knowledge of HTML and CSS to recreate each component one by one, making sure that the positioning, padding, margins, and font sizes were consistent with the mockup.

Throughout the process, I encountered some challenges, such as aligning elements correctly and ensuring that the website was responsive on different devices. However, these challenges helped me to learn new techniques and strategies to overcome them. In the end, I was able to create a website that closely resembled the original mockup, which was a significant achievement for me. This experience helped me to develop my HTML and CSS skills, and I'm proud of what I was able to accomplish.

Overall, it was a valuable learning experience that taught me the importance of attention to detail, precision, and problem-solving skills when writing code.

Avatar Icon Design

Creating an avatar icon project is a great opportunity to showcase my design and illustration skills. The project requires designing an animal avatar icon in three different dimensions, with varying levels of detail and complexity. As an illustrator, this task is a challenging yet exciting one. I will need to utilize my expertise in vector format, scaling, and color grading to create an outstanding avatar icon.

Starting with the small icon, I will have to be creative and come up with a design that is easy to recognize, despite its limited size and few details. The black and white color scheme will demand that I focus more on the animal's shape and outline to ensure that it is still identifiable even in small dimensions.

As I progress to the medium-sized icon, the challenge increases with the addition of color and more details. I will have to decide which colors best suit my animal avatar and bring it to life. The design must still be recognizable and appeal to users even with added complexity.

Finally, the large-sized icon is where I can let my creativity run wild. With gradients and more elaborate designs, I can create a unique and stunning avatar icon that will be sure to catch the user's eye.

Overall, creating an avatar icon project is an excellent opportunity for me as an illustrator to showcase my skills and push myself creatively.

Branding Design

For my branding design assignment, I had to create a visual identity for a fictional company, which was a challenging but exciting project for me. I had to consider various aspects of the company, including its target audience, values, and mission, to create a cohesive and memorable brand.

Overall, this branding design assignment challenged me to think creatively and strategically, and I am proud of the final result. I learned a lot about the importance of developing a cohesive visual identity and how it can impact a company's success. I am excited to apply these skills to future design projects and continue to improve my abilities as a designer.
